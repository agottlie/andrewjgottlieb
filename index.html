ABOUT ME

I’m a passionate web developer looking to combine my creativity and operations background in a collaborative environment. My experience in everything from the advertising world to playing in a band gives me the adaptability needed to work in a fast-paced workplace. I’m eager to learn, to lead, and to inspire.


I graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a Bachelor's degree in Sociology and a minor in Economics. Shortly after I moved to New York City, and have spent time in the startup world as well as the corporate world, working with everything from finance to augmented reality. When not in front of a computer, you'll probably find me behind my drum kit or biking around Brooklyn.


Above all else, I'm a problem solver. I love tinkering around with code to improve functionality, increase efficiency, and add cool features.
